My Story

Simply for the reason that I don't feel safe because I am constantly living in fear that I will be kidnapped or sexually assualted or worse. The police and society say they do their best to protect the citizens of the city but I think it's a lie because because if something were to happen to me no one else would be blamed but me. This is what our society has come to.
